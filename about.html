    • About Queen of Martyrs High School
    Welcome to Queen of Martyrs High School, a prestigious educational institution with a rich history and a strong commitment to academic excellence, character development, and Catholic values. Our Founding Queen of Martyrs High School was founded by the late Kaloli Kiwanuka Charles, a visionary leader dedicated to providing quality education in the spirit of Catholic values. His enduring legacy lives on in our institution, and his commitment to nurturing the minds and spirits of our students remains at the heart of everything we do. Our Catholic Heritage As a Catholic-founded school, Queen of Martyrs High School is firmly rooted in the teachings and traditions of the Catholic Church. We are devoted to fostering an environment where faith, morals, and spirituality are an integral part of the educational experience. Our mission is to empower students to grow not only academically but also in their understanding of their faith and their capacity to serve the community. Our Commitment to Excellence At Queen of Martyrs High School, we are dedicated to providing a top-notch education that equips our students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for success in today's dynamic world. Our experienced faculty members are passionate about nurturing the intellectual, moral, and personal growth of every student. We offer a wide range of academic programs and extracurricular activities that cater to diverse interests and abilities. Our Vision Our vision is to be a leading institution in the region, recognized for our commitment to academic excellence, spiritual growth, and community service. We aim to instill in our students a sense of purpose, a strong moral compass, and the tools to excel in their chosen paths, all while fostering a deep connection to their Catholic faith. Join Our Community We invite you to explore our website and discover the myriad opportunities and resources available at Queen of Martyrs High School. Whether you are a prospective student, parent, or member of our extended community, we look forward to welcoming you into our family. Come, be a part of our journey, and together, let us continue to uphold the legacy of our founder, the late Kaloli Kiwanuka Charles, as we work towards nurturing the leaders and citizens of tomorrow. Thank you for your interest in Queen of Martyrs High School. We are excited to embark on this educational journey with you.

    OUR GREAT FOUNDER

    Queen of Martyrs: A Legacy of Catholic Education Established by the Late Kaloli Kiwanuka In the heart of our community lies a distinguished institution that has, for decades, been a shining example of Catholic education. Queen of Martyrs, a name synonymous with academic excellence and unwavering faith, was founded by the late Kaloli Kiwanuka with a vision to establish a strong foundation for Catholic education. The story of Queen of Martyrs is a testament to the remarkable dedication and foresight of its founder, Kaloli Kiwanuka. Recognizing the vital role that education plays in shaping the minds and hearts of young individuals, he embarked on a journey to create a haven of learning deeply rooted in the Catholic tradition. Founded on principles of faith, knowledge, and community, Queen of Martyrs has thrived as an institution where students not only receive a high-quality education but also embrace the moral and spiritual values that are at the core of Catholic teaching. The school's commitment to nurturing young minds and souls has made it an integral part of our community. Kaloli Kiwanuka's legacy lives on through Queen of Martyrs. His vision and unwavering determination have left an indelible mark on the education landscape. It is a place where students are not only academically enriched but also instilled with the timeless virtues of compassion, integrity, and service. Today, Queen of Martyrs remains a beacon of Catholic education, carrying forward the torch lit by its founder. The school stands as a living testament to the enduring impact that one individual's vision can have on the lives of countless students and the broader community. We are proud to be associated with Queen of Martyrs, a school that continues to shape the future while honoring its rich heritage. Queen of Martyrs: Where faith and education converge, thanks to the visionary spirit of the late Kaloli Kiwanuka, who recognized the importance of establishing a Catholic educational legacy.
